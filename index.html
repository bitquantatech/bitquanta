Why Traditional Inspection Falls Short

  • Manual visual inspection is subjective and can vary between inspectors.
  • Results are often not repeatable over time or across different teams.
  • Large or complex surfaces are hard to inspect thoroughly.
  • Missed contamination or defects can lead to delays, rejected loads, and financial loss.
  • Working in enclosed or hazardous environments introduces significant safety risks.

From Images to Quantitative, Safe Decisions

  • Objective, data-driven results instead of subjective impressions
  • Up to ~95% detection accuracy for contamination/defect scenarios
  • Remote and automated inspection, improving operator safety
  • Easy integration into existing industrial and logistics workflows
  • Reduced operational and financial risk through earlier and more reliable detection
